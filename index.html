About me

I am an aspiring data analyst seeking a remote opportunity where I can leverage my skills in data manipulation using spreadsheets, R, and SQL. With a solid foundation in Python from academic projects, I began my journey by self-teaching SQL while refining my Excel and statistical knowledge. To further hone my expertise, I completed the Google Data Analytics Bootcamp, gaining industry-standard insights and techniques.

I hold a First Class MChem degree from the University of Sussex and the Royal Society of Chemistry prize for the MChem best presentation in the UK Downland section. During my studies, I completed several laboratory and non-laboratory projects. These experiences instilled in me a strong sense of scientific rigour and honed my analytical skills which are directly transferable to data analysis, offering a unique perspective not commonly found in those who began their careers exclusively in data analysis. Additionally, my industry experience in Water Quality Analysis has further enhanced my analytical skills and attention to detail, compelling me to apply those principles in a very fast-paced environment. I developed practical expertise in creating visualizations with Python and handling various types of data, both experimental and from literature. These experiences have equipped me with a strong foundation in data analysis and problem-solving.

All of the above have solidified my confidence in my ability to contribute effectively to any data analysis project.
