Project Summary

This project demonstrates my ability to turn raw data into interactive, insightful visualizations using Power BI. I created a dynamic dashboard with insights on average salaries by job title, demographic distribution, and programming language preferences. The drill-down functionality allows users to view average salaries by industry within each job title for precise benchmarking.

Key trends include the prevalence of survey respondents from the United States, India, and the United Kingdom, and top job priorities such as "Better Salary," "Remote Work," and "Good Work/Life Balance." The dashboard also explores the difficulty of entering the field, with many finding it "Neither easy nor difficult."

Visuals like bar charts, KPI indicators, treemaps, and gauge charts deliver clear, actionable insights. This project showcases my level of expertise in Power BI, data wrangling, and creating scalable, interactive solutions for data-driven decision-making.
